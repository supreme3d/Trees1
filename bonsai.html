Bonsai

The Bonsai trees are some of the "smallest" trees around. Bonsai trees are small trees that are cultivated in a way that they don't have enough nutrition to grow into big trees so this means that any tree can be cultivated in the way of the Bonsai and you will get a cute little Bonsai tree. If you gave the Bonsai tree enough nutrition and space to grow it could grow into a big tree like one you would see at your local park.
